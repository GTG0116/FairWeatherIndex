Scoring Methodology

Feels Like & Temperature (30% + 10%)
Scored relative to seasonal normals for the location. "Average" = near-normal for the time of year. Deviating toward the comfort zone (cooler in summer, warmer in winter) scores higher; deviating away scores lower.
Precipitation Chance (25%)
<10% Excellent · 10–20% Good · 20–40% Average · 40–60% Somewhat OK · 60–80% Poor · >80% Extremely Poor
Wind Speed (15%)
<5 mph Excellent · 5–10 Good · 10–20 Average · 20–30 Somewhat OK · 30–40 Poor · >40 Extremely Poor
Relative Humidity (10%)
35–55% Excellent · 55–65% Good · 65–75% Average · 75–85% Somewhat OK · 85–93% Poor · >93% Extremely Poor
Cloud Cover (10%)
<15% Excellent · 15–30% Good · 30–55% Average · 55–75% Somewhat OK · 75–90% Poor · >90% Extremely Poor
